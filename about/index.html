Our Vision

The vision of Hour of Grace came about with the direction of God given through His servant Apostle Vida Owusu who is the founder and General Overseer of the Ministry.

Hour of Grace Ministry International exists to bring people into a living relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, teaching, and compassionate outreach across the United Kingdom and beyond.

About Our Ministry

The notion behind the ministry is to proclaim the good news throughout the world. Hour of Grace Family Chapel Int. is committed to organising youth and women empowerment conferences. We aim to teach the word of God in the United Kingdom and the world as a whole.

We are also committed to assisting the less privileged and do street evangelism by sharing Christian flyers as well as having friendly chats with people we encounter on the streets. Lastly we also teach youth maths, English and music.

Evangelism

Street outreach, Christian literature, and friendly conversations with our community.

Empowerment

Youth and women's conferences designed to equip and inspire.

Education

Maths, English, science, and music classes for young people every Saturday.

Compassion

Supporting the less privileged through practical acts of love and service.
