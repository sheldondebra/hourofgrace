Church Recruitment Opportunity

Hour of Grace Family Chapel invites passionate individuals seeking to serve as religious workers and ministers of religion in the United Kingdom. If you are committed to faith-based service and meet UK sponsorship licence requirements, we welcome your application.

Roles we are recruiting for include:

  • Instrumentalists
  • Pastors & Evangelists
  • Bible Teachers
  • Women's Fellowship Leaders
  • Charity & Welfare Officers
  • Proclamation Officers
  • Churchwardens & Caretakers

A valid DBS check is required for all applicants.
